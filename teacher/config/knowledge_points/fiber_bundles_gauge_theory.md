# Fiber Bundles And Gauge Theory

## Core Bridge

Electromagnetism -> gauge potential -> local symmetry -> principal bundle -> connection -> curvature -> Yang-Mills.

## Required Checks

- Explain why a gauge potential is connection-like rather than an ordinary vector field.
- Distinguish gauge invariance from gauge covariance.
- Explain the local form of curvature: $F=dA+A\\wedge A$.
- Contrast U(1) gauge theory with non-abelian gauge theory.

## Feynman Task

Explain why the term $A\\wedge A$ appears in non-abelian field strength.
