# Quantum Field Theory

## Core Bridge

Classical mechanics -> action principle -> fields -> canonical quantization/path integrals -> particles as field excitations.

## Required Checks

- Derive the field Euler-Lagrange equation from an action.
- Explain what a path integral sums over.
- Connect symmetries to conserved currents through Noether theorem.
- Distinguish fields, particles, operators, and states.

## Feynman Task

Explain why QFT treats fields as fundamental and particles as excitations.
