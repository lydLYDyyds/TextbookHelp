# Group And Representation Theory

## Core Bridge

Linear algebra -> group actions -> representations -> Lie algebras -> quantum symmetries.

## Required Checks

- Distinguish a group homomorphism from a Lie algebra homomorphism.
- Explain why a representation lets a symmetry act on a vector space.
- Use SO(3) and SU(2) as the main bridge to angular momentum and spin.
- State what irreducibility means in terms of invariant subspaces.

## Feynman Task

Explain representation theory to someone who only knows matrices and symmetries.
