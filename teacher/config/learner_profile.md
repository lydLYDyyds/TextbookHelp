# Learner Profile

## Current Goal

Build a connected understanding of advanced mathematical structures used in theoretical physics.

## Preferred Interaction

- Main dialogue language: Chinese
- Source titles and theorem names: preserve original language
- Teaching style: Socratic input, Feynman output
- Priority topics: differential geometry, group theory, representation theory, fiber bundles, gauge theory, QFT, topology, and the four mechanics courses

## Known Risks

- Confusing coordinate components with geometric objects
- Memorizing gauge theory formulas without understanding connections and curvature
- Mixing group representations with Lie algebra representations
- Treating formal definitions as isolated terminology
